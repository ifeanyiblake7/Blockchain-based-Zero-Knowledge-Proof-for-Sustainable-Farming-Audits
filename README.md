# 🌾 Blockchain-based Zero-Knowledge Proof for Sustainable Farming Audits

Welcome to the coolest way to audit sustainable farming practices on the blockchain! This project lets farmers prove compliance with sustainability standards using zero-knowledge proofs on the Stacks blockchain.

## ✨ Features

🔒 Prove compliance without revealing sensitive data  
📋 Register farms with unique IDs and commitments  
✅ Verify sustainability certifications instantly  
🏅 Issue digital certificates for compliant farms  
🔍 Transparent verification for regulators and consumers  
🚫 Immutable audit trails to prevent tampering  
⚖️ Resolve disputes over audit results  
📊 Share anonymized sustainability metrics  

## 🛠 How It Works

**For Farmers**

- Register your farm with a unique ID and sustainability commitments  
- Generate a zero-knowledge proof of compliance  
- Submit the proof to receive a digital certificate  

Boom! Your farm’s sustainability is now verified on the blockchain  

**For Verifiers**

- Use get-farm-details to view farm registration info  
- Call verify-compliance to confirm sustainability standards  
- Access analytics for public sustainability trends  

That’s it! Instant, privacy-preserving verification
